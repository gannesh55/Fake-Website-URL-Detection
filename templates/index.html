About PhishGuardian

PhishGuardian is a machine learning-based system designed to detect phishing websites with high accuracy. It analyzes key features of a URL, such as its structure, domain, and security indicators, to predict whether the site is safe or suspicious. Our goal is to protect users from cyber threats by providing real-time assessments of web links.
